üí° Key Insights from These Visualizations:

  • Sex matters MOST: "Women and children first" policy was real - females had 4x survival rate
  • Wealth equals safety: First class passengers had 3x the survival rate of third class
  • Age distribution: Ship had passengers from babies to 80-year-olds, average ~30 years
  • Pattern recognition: These features will be important for our ML model!
# Visualization 1: Survival by Sex sns.barplot(x='Sex', y='Survived', data=train) plt.title('Survival Rate by Sex') plt.show() # Visualization 2: Survival by Class sns.barplot(x='Pclass', y='Survived', data=train) plt.title('Survival Rate by Passenger Class') plt.show() # Visualization 3: Age Distribution train['Age'].hist(bins=30) plt.title('Age Distribution') plt.show()
